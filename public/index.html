Story

An NFT Collection for Those Who Really Care

The metaverse might sound interesting, but accessing the metaverse is only possible if we have a planet to live on.

Curious Penguins is a collection of 1,111 unique NFTs stored as ERC-721 on the Ethereum blockchain. The images are saved in the SVG file format so they can be easily scaled to any size without distortion.

Not only can holders select unique traits for their Penguin based on a particular field of scientific study, but they also choose a custom background and their Penguin’s activity.
